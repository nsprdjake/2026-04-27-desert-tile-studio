Built: **Desert Tile Studio** — a polished one-file Palm Springs pattern generator.

Why it’s cool:
- Makes seamless repeat tiles with motel sunbursts, pool tiles, cactus blooms, terrazzo confetti, and casino sparkle motifs
- Includes curated neon/desert palettes, seed-based variations, and quick shuffle/evolve controls
- Exports both a square tile PNG and a portrait poster PNG, so the same idea can jump from texture to promo graphic fast
- Feels playful, but it also has a real path into packaging, wallpaper, merch, print backgrounds, and social content
